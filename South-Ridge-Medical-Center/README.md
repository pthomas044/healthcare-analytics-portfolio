# 🏥 South Ridge Medical Center

## Project Overview

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to evaluate hospital operational performance and investigate Emergency Department wait times using simulated healthcare operations data.

---

## Business Problem

Leadership identified prolonged Emergency Department wait times despite patient volumes comparable to other departments.

The objective was to investigate operational metrics and identify potential contributors to delays.

---

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Microsoft Excel

---

## Skills Demonstrated

- Dashboard Design
- KPI Development
- Data Cleaning
- Healthcare Operations Analytics
- Data Storytelling
- Executive Reporting

---

## Key Findings

- Emergency Department experienced the highest average wait times.
- Provider staffing increased appropriately during peak demand.
- Declining bed availability aligned more closely with elevated wait times than staffing levels alone.
- Additional operational investigation is recommended before implementing staffing changes.
